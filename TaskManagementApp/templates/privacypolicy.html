Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Blunotions™, we are dedicated to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in the handling of your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices and your rights regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

2. Data We Collect

  • Personal Data – Information such as your name, email address, phone number, and government-issued identification.
  • Financial Data – Payment details and cryptocurrency wallet addresses necessary for transactions.
  • Device & Usage Data – Data including IP addresses, geolocation, and session logs to improve user experience.
  • User-Generated Content – Comments, reviews, and interactions provided on our platform.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To verify user identity and ensure AML/KYC compliance.
  • To process cryptocurrency transactions and manage donations.
  • To prevent fraud, enforce policies, and enhance security.

4. Data Sharing

Blunotions™ does not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Regulatory authorities, as required by law
  • Payment processors to facilitate transactions
  • Security providers to protect our users

5. Security Measures

  • Implementation of end-to-end encryption
  • Mandatory two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Strict access controls to protect sensitive data
