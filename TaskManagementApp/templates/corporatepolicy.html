Corporate Policies

Last Updated: 2025

1. Introduction

At Blunotions™, we are committed to conducting business with integrity, transparency, and ethical responsibility. These Corporate Policies outline our core values, compliance standards, and operational expectations for all stakeholders, including employees, contractors, partners, and affiliates.

2. Code of Conduct

  • Honesty, respect, and professionalism in all interactions
  • Strict compliance with applicable laws and company policies
  • Avoidance of conflicts of interest and insider dealings
  • Zero tolerance for fraud, corruption, or bribery

3. Anti-Discrimination & Workplace Equality

Blunotions™ strictly prohibits discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, age, disability, religion, or any other protected characteristic.

4. Cybersecurity & Data Protection

  • Mandatory two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Encryption protocols for data protection
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Prohibited use of unauthorized third-party software

5. Intellectual Property & Confidentiality

All intellectual property created under Blunotions™ is legally protected. Employees and contractors must:

  • Respect copyright laws and company trade secrets
  • Not disclose confidential information
  • Report IP violations immediately
